FAQ Accordion
What makes GUAC different from a vector database?

Vector databases approximate relevance. GUAC uses structured knowledge graphs and addressable conversation history for deterministic retrieval.

Can I self-host GUAC?

Yes. The core CLI is MIT-licensed and runs locally. Commercial tiers add cloud sync and enterprise controls.
