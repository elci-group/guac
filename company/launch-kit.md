## Boilerplate

**Short (50 words)**

GUAC (Git + Groq Augmented Cognition) is the memory infrastructure for AI systems. It replaces fuzzy vector search and prompt stuffing with deterministic, Git-backed knowledge graphs and conversation memory that models can reason over.

**Long (100 words)**

GUAC (Git + Groq Augmented Cognition) gives AI systems a persistent, structured, version-controlled memory. Every conversation turn becomes a Git commit. Stable facts live in a YAML knowledge graph. Messages are scored on importance, novelty, recency, and repetition, and old conversations are recursively compressed into living summaries. The result is deterministic retrieval, auditable history, and ultra-low-latency reasoning — an operating system for thought, not just a chatbot with a longer context window.
